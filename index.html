Welcome to Gotta Go Fast!

Gotta Go Fast (GGs) Society is RGU's unofficial speedrunning society! Whether it be tearing up the track in Mario Kart, dodging monstrosities in Little Nightmares, or living your best cat life in Stray, we play everything. The only rule? Gotta go fast!
